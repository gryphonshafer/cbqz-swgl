How It Works

A Season of Study, A Year of Memories

Each fall, your team of three begins a deep dive into a book (or books) of the Bible. Over the course of the season, you study a few chapters each month — working through repetition, discussion, and memorization until the words become second nature. God’s Word doesn’t just pass through your mind; it embeds into your heart.

Once a month, teams gather for a quiz meet — a structured competition where you work together to demonstrate what you’ve learned. The atmosphere is energetic and encouraging, built around healthy competition, teamwork, and fun. Win or lose, you leave sharper than you arrived.
